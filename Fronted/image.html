DOG TRAINING COURSES

Whether you have a young pup, older dog or a rescue, PetSmart training classes can help them learn how to behave appropriately at home and on-the-go.

Age Requirements Puppy classes are for dogs 10 weeks to 5 months old. Beginner, Intermediate and Advance courses are for dogs 5 months or older. Vaccinations DPP (Distemper, Parvo & Parainfluenza) & Rabies vaccinations are required for any dog over 4 months old. Proof must be provided in the first class, in order to participate.
